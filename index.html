Appetite

iOS App that trains you to eat healthier.

Appetite is an iOS application where users play a game swiping images of food. In the proccess they train their minds to eat better.


Our partners conducted a study, using a program written in MatLab, where they tested the effectiveness of a stop signal task related to healthy and unhealthy food on oneâ€™s eating habits. Our problem is to create a game-like iOS application that recreates this study in a more fun way, while simultaneously reproducing the studies results.

We plan to develop an iOS application that uses stop signal tasks to subconsciously train the users to eat more healthily, based loosely on the Matlab program of our partner. The application will have two game modes. The first is a go, no-go game and the other is stop signal task game. We intend to be conscious in our design decisions in order to maintain the psychological study and benefits. Simultaneously, we hope to liven the application up a bit by making it more game-like and thus more attractive to the average person hoping to eat healthier.
